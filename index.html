About

40 years of Naturéa perfumes

Naturéa is a French company specializing in perfume production since 1984. Founded by a family passionate about nature, the brand is committed to using only natural and organic ingredients for the creation of its fragrances. Naturéa works closely with local producers and artisans to guarantee the quality and authenticity of its perfumes. The company offers a wide range of perfumes for men and women, as well as home products such as candles and diffusers. Naturéa is recognized for its ethical and environmentally friendly approach, making it a brand of choice for consumers who are mindful of their health and the planet.
